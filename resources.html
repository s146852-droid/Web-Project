Tips for Building a Good Schedule

  • Avoid scheduling more than three heavy courses on the same day.
  • Try to keep at least one “study block” free during the day.
  • Balance lab sessions so they are not all on the same day.
  • Use colors in your timetable to separate core courses from electives.
